SQUARE TWO

BLACKPINK

November 1, 2016

BLACKPINK is a South Korean girl group formed by YG Entertainment and the first girl group that debuted under the entertainment agency in seven years after 2NE1. The group consists of four members: Jennie, Lisa, Jisoo, and Rosé. They officially debuted on August 8, 2016 with the single album Square One.
